Target Audience

The target audience is the people of Fayetteville, Arkansas. More specifically those who are looking to be involed in their community and support local businesses. Usually this audience is comprised of young adults who are looking to grow their career. However, there is a large population of middle aged people in Fayetteville meaning that I will focus more on that generation to help them get motivated to get involved.

Personas

Person #1

Name:

Darleen Johnson

Job Title:

American Restaurant Equipment Company Owner

Demographics

43 Years Old, Single/Divorced, Mother to two children, Associates in Business Management

Goals and Tasks:

She has a strong personality and likes to get things done. She takes no lip from anybody and always keeps her promises and accomplishes her goal. She is focused and helping her community and has a passion for small businesses. Manages a restaurant equipment store that helps local restaurants, Reviews and approves requests/supervises other staff

Environment

She is good with people and people seem to gravitate towards her. She is an excellent coordinator of tasks and delegator. She is a natural leader. Not very comfortable with technology and prefers not to even use social media, however, she uses her computer daily to moderate requests and approve restaurants to send equipment to.

Person #2

Name:

Derek Jenkins

Job Title:

CEO at Mycelium Networks

Demographics

29 Years Old, Married, Masters in Business Analytics

Goals and Tasks:

Derek is extremel smart, quiet and analytical. His dream is just to provide for his family and work hard through his life. He is focused a lot of family life and has a good sense of community but doesn't get involved as much as he would like.

Environment

He is quiet but hard working. It is often hard to tell if he is really interested in talking to you. He works well with others and seeks to help others around him, especially in his position as CEO.

Scenarios

Jacob is a young business owner and wants a little more speaking power in his business community. He has concerns and proposals about education and government regulations in his business and needs support.

Aubrey Owns a Large business that helps the University of Arkansas in Fayetteville but feels as though she struggles to connect with the locals. She needs help in better connecting with locals regardless of the size of her business.

Kennedy and Lincoln just got married and started a small side business to sell jam to the community. They need somewhere they can go and find community events and participate to sell jam there and get to know their community.

Sam is really passionate about business and wants to help and provide help to businesses around him. he owns an accounting company that helps business and needs to get involed in as many business who may need his help.

Hailey is business owner and is getting extremely frustrated with other business opportunities falling through because those businesses do not take her business seriously. She needs a place she can go to gain credibility and make her business seem more legitimate.
