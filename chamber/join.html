Membership Levels and Benefits

Basic Membership

As a Basic Member of our Chamber of Commerce, you will enjoy the following benefits:

  • Access to networking events and business seminars
  • Listing in the online business directory
  • Subscription to our monthly newsletter
  • 10% discount on chamber-sponsored events

Silver Membership

Upgrade to our Silver Membership to receive enhanced benefits:

  • All benefits of Basic Membership
  • Premium listing in the online business directory
  • Recognition on our website and social media platforms
  • Invitation to exclusive networking events
  • 15% discount on chamber-sponsored events

Gold Membership

Our prestigious Gold Membership offers the highest level of benefits for businesses:

  • All benefits of Silver Membership
  • Featured placement in the online business directory
  • Opportunity to showcase your business at chamber events
  • Promotion in our newsletter and press releases
  • 20% discount on chamber-sponsored events
